INFECTION GAMEPLAY RULES

1

Both players enter their names. They can choose between two styles of gameplay:

  • Automatic - A die is provided on the screen to roll.
  • Manual - The player rolls their own physical die, and enters the number on the screen.
2

An infectious blob is automatically placed in the center of the 9x9 grid, at (5,5). Each player has two "headquarters" located on opposite corners of the board.

3

Each player takes turns rolling the die. If it's a:

  • 1: Blob Moves Up
  • 2: Blob Moves Right
  • 3: Blob Moves Down
  • 4: Blob Moves Left
  • 5: None
  • 6: All adjacent cells to the infected blob are infected.

In the manual version, once the user rolls the physical die, they should enter the number rolled into the following box.

4

The player that has none of their headquarters infected by the infectious blob wins. If both players have headquarters that are infected at the same time, it is a tie.
